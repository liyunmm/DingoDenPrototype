Dingo Den
Animal Rescue

Dingo Den Animal Rescue is a non-profit volunteer based Australian charity. Aiming to preserve Australia's dingo/dingo-hybrid population through rehabilitation and legislative measures.

Dingo Den is registered through the Australian Charities and Not-for-Profits Commission (ACNC).

Dingo Den relies on the generosity of the public encouraging those available to volunteer with our programs. Your help ensures a safer future for Australia ºs most iconic fauna.
